Become a Champion

Join our mission to empower survivors of SGBV by becoming a Champion on the Heal platform. As a Champion, you will provide peer-to-peer support to others in need, drawing from your own experience and healing journey.

Training Program Overview

  • Understanding SGBV and its impacts
  • Basics of mental health and trauma-informed care
  • Effective communication and active listening skills
  • Establishing trust and confidentiality
  • Peer support techniques
  • Using the Heal platform to provide support
  • Referral processes for professional therapy

Training Details

Duration: 4 weeks

Format: Online sessions, self-paced modules, and interactive workshops

Eligibility: SGBV survivors who have completed their healing journey and are passionate about helping others

How to Enroll

Follow these steps to start your journey as a Champion:

  1. Complete the online application form.
  2. Attend an initial interview to assess readiness.
  3. Begin your training and mentorship program.
